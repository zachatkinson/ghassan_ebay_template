How it Works + Shipping

Ghassan Automotive wants to provide a stress-free and convenient experience. If you need an engine replacement or want to upgrade the one you already have, Ghassan Automotive can do that for you. The process is zero-hassle, from picking up the vehicle or engine to rebuilding it and returning it to you. Engines are usually in stock and ready to ship.

Fully insured vehicle transportation rates are estimated at 85+ cents per mile, or engine shipment is a flat rate of $595 each way; shipping costs may vary depending on accommodation. In-house engine installations are available with labor as low as $2,995.

You must contact Ghassan Automotive before shipping is arranged. There is a $1,000 core charge that is not included in the price and is refunded once the core engine is received. Core engines can be shipped to Ghassan Automotive in advance. All core engines must be in a rebuildable condition to be accepted. All payments must be made via certified funds.

The engine ships as a long block with no accessories.
